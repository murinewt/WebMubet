MEMBERSHIP, RENEWAL & CESSATION

(A) Membership shall be open to individuals and corporate. It
shall comprise of:
   i. Full Membership
a) Must be an undergraduate of any branch that is affiliated to the ministry, who ascribes to the faith and confess God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit subject to Article 5.
b) Must be committed to the ministry activities: missions, prayer retreats, conventions and fellowships among others.
   ii. Special/Members by Partnership
Shall be open to individuals, ministries, churches, societies and organizations who have not been members in any of the affiliated branches but have partnered closely with the ministry and its activities and is/are willing to continue doing so.
  iii. Associate/Alumni Membership
Shall be open to all former students of the affiliated branches upon signing of an associate/alumni membership form and who uphold the Christian Faith subject to article 5.

(B): Renewal of Membership.
Membership in the above category (i) shall be renewed at the beginning of every academic year upon payment of the registration fee evidenced by membership card while category (ii) shall be renewed after every two years after signing of the form. (iii) shall be renewed after every five years.

(C): Cessation of Membership
Membership in categories (i) above shall cease in the event of a written completion or termination of studies at the affiliated branches, while in category (ii) above shall cease in the event of being inactive as deemed necessary by the NGC. The category described in category (iii) above shall cease upon submitting a written resignation and/or upon a careful and judicious exercise of power by the Executive Committee.

DOCTRINAL BASIS

The doctrinal basis shall be the fundamental truths of Christianity including the following:
i. The unity of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit in
the Godhead,
ii. The sovereignty of God in creation, redemption, revelation, and final judgment,
iii. The divine inspiration, entire trustworthiness of the Holy scripture, as originally given and its supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct,
iv. The universal sinfulness and guilt of all men since the fall, rendering them subject to God’s wrath and condemnation,
v. Redemption from the guilt, penalty, dominion and pollution of sin solely through the sacrificial death (as our representative and substitute) of the Lord Jesus Christ, the incarnate Son of God,
vi. The bodily resurrection of the Lord Jesus Christ from the dead and His ascension to
the right hand of God the Father,
vii. The justification of the sinner by the grace of God through faith alone,
viii. The presence and power of the Holy Spirit in the work
of the regeneration,
ix. The indwelling and work of the Holy Spirit in the believer,
x. The one holy universal church to which all true believers belong,
xi. The expectation of the personal return of the Lord Jesus Christ.

GOVERNANCE

Joint Coordinating Council (JCC)

It shall consist of the Associate Governing Council and the Student Governing Council :
Shall appoint a director who will chair it,
Shall appoint other leaders of the council at their liberty,
Shall appoint the Board of Trustees,
Shall have powers to dismiss the director and,
Shall have powers to dismiss the ALC in accordance to Article 10, f(ii),
Shall have a calendar of activities to help govern the branches,
The decision of JCC shall override all the decisions made by the branches and  the regions,
Shall have powers to open and run a bank account.

National(Student) Governing Council(NGC)

Its the main decision making body,
It shall form the Executive Committee,
Has powers to open and run a bank account.

Associate Link Coordinator

i)Description:
Shall:
be an associate,
have the advisory role and not participate in voting,
be there to link the students body and the associates body,
be nominated  by the JCC and be allowed to sit in the JCC,AGC and NGC meetings,
serve for a period of 3 years.

ii)Cessation
Shall cease to be the ALC if:
Ø  His/her term expires,
Ø  Post falls vacant due to death,resignation
and/or contravention of doctrinal basis as per Article 5,
Ø  If he/she remains inactive for a period
deemed appropriate by the JCC.

Associate Governing Council (AGC)

Composed of the associates/alumnis,
Shall appoint leaders to this council at their liberty,
Shall have an advisory role,
Shall have powers to open and run their bank account.

Regional Governing Council (RGC)

Shall be involved in recruiting more branches into the Ministry,
Shall organise inter-branch visits and activities,
Shall be involved in strengthening the branches and helping them achieve their objectives,
Shall have powers to open and run their bank account,
Shall be supported by member branches.

 Branch Executive Committee

Should not contradict the activity of the National body,
Has the autonomy to come up with its charter that does not contradict the Ministry’s constitution and any contradiction thereof shall be null and void to the extent of the contravention.
