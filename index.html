WHO WE ARE

Mid-Eastern United Brethren Evangilistic Team
It’s an Evangelistic team which comprises of campus students from Embu, Isiolo, Tharaka Nithi, Meru counties and North Eastern region, and any other student willing to join who ascribes to Christian faith as found in scriptures and is willing to show commitment to the MUBET.

VISION

To make Christ known and transform the society through the holistic gospel.

MISSION

The ministry is prayerfully committed to proclaiming the gospel of Christ through preaching, training, nurturing and practicing the Word of God.

WHAT WE DO

To proclaim the holistic gospel of Christ in every part of Mid-Eastern region of Kenya and beyond

To promote unity in the body of Christ

To encourage interdenominational fellowships

Make disciples and nurture Christians

Sensitize members into mission work in their heed to God’s calling
